Einar Wigen

Oslo, Norway
Head of Research
University of Oslo's Department of Culture, Religion, Asian and Middle Eastern Studies

Einar Wigen is professor of Middle Eastern studies and Head of Research at the University of Oslo's Department of Culture, Religion, Asian and Middle Eastern Studies. Empirically, his research interests range from early Turkic state-formation via plague in the Ottoman Empire to contemporary Turkish politics. On the theory side, he works on temporality, conceptual history, crises, polity systems, and political legitimacy.
